Investment
==========

Now that we have established the foundation for reason and a proper frame for interpreting information, we can begin with our analysis of the material world and how to navigate it.

The most important thing you need to do to be successful in this world is to make good choices. Once you make good choices, you have to back them up with hard work. In order to do both of these, you need to approach problems in the right way with a desire to solve them, improve yourself and be successful.
